ThisMovieIs

A place for true movie lovers. For those who have watched hundreds of films and one day realized that most modern movies are just repetitions of each other — the same plot twists, cliché characters, and predictable endings. We created a platform where details are valued — because it’s the details that distinguish mediocrity from real cinema.

ThisMovieIs offers four ways to discover films:

1. Search through movie selections made by cinephiles.
2. Search by top ratings based on cinephile votes.
3. Search in the “New Releases” category — usually films currently playing in theaters.
4. Traditional search by categories and titles, powered by custom algorithms.

Below is detailed information about each type of search.

Search through movie selections made by cinephiles. Curated

It’s no secret that some people are truly passionate about cinema — they watch movies almost every day and have seen thousands of them. Over time, they develop a refined sense of taste, easily distinguishing mediocre films from truly high-quality and meaningful ones. To find such people, simply visit the “Top Users” section and choose a cinephile whose taste matches your own.

The main difference between this type of search and traditional platforms like YouTube or IMDb is the personal approach. Here, selections are made not by algorithms or the averaged ratings of thousands of users, but by real people who carefully choose each film. On YouTube or IMDb, a high rating usually means that the movie is simply “okay” and liked by the majority.

Here — it’s the opposite: the top lists will differ greatly from mainstream media, as they reflect the personal preferences of true movie enthusiasts.

Most importantly, every user’s top list is unique. While other platforms feature the same lists for months or even years, ours are dynamic and ever-changing: tastes evolve, new discoveries appear, and rankings update along with them.
